Join us to #RUNLagos

04, Feb 2023.
Lagos, Nigeria.

Race Info

Starting Point

The starting point will be at the entrance of the National Stadium, Surulere, Lagos (on Western Avenue and opposite Teslim Balogun Stadium).

Start Time: 7am

Finish Point

The finishing point will be at the Eko Atlantic City, Ahmadu Bello Way, Victoria Island, Lagos.

Time Stops: 1pm

Awards

All finishers of the race will recieve a medal and a certificate.

PLEASE NOTE:

Participants are advised to come with proof of identification when coming to collect their running numbers. Peradventure, participants intend to have their running number collected on their behalf; the person collecting must come to our office with the printed out copy of the form or the final confirmation received by e-mail. They must have proof of their identity in order to do this.
